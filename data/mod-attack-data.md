## Key Observations

**Ballistic Missiles:** Opening salvo of 137 on Day 1, rapidly declining to single digits by Day 5. Day 7 saw a minor uptick (7→9), breaking monotonic decay. This likely reflects residual mobile TEL launches rather than a sustained rebound.

**Drones:** Peak daily launch of 332 on Day 2, stabilizing around 110-130/day from Day 4 onwards. Production-rate-limited pattern suggests steady-state manufacturing capacity of ~130/day.

**Cruise Missiles:** Sporadic deployment — 8 total over 7 days. Not a primary attack vector.

**Interception Rates:** Consistently above 92%, ranging from 92.7% to 93.6%. THAAD and Patriot systems performing within design parameters.

## Confirmed Casualties

As of March 7: **3 fatalities, 112 injured** (cumulative). Remarkably low given attack volume, attributable to >92% interception rates.
